Ideas for incorporating mindfulness into daily life

• Practise mindfulness while cleaning your teeth, washing dishes, standing in a line, or waiting for the kettle to boil.

• Bring mindful awareness to part of your commute - walking, sitting on the train, or waiting at a traffic light.

• Listen to your favourite music mindfully, directing all your attention toward the experience of listening.

• Use a sound that happens regularly as a modern 'mindfulness bell', a cue to remind you to practise. It could be a car horn, laughter, phone notification, or a door shutting.

• Set a lock screen on your phone with a picture that prompts you to be mindful.

• Take 3 mindful breaths first thing when you wake up, and last thing before you go to sleep at night.

• When you notice discomfort or tension in your body, imagine yourself breathing space around it, then releasing tension with your exhale.
