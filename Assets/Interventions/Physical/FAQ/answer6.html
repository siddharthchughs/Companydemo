I feel sore after exercising

The first time we do an activity, some level of muscle soreness is to be expected, although this shouldn’t interfere with function.

If you are experiencing soreness, it should subside within 1-3 days.

Many people find that any discomfort is at its worst around 48 hours after exercise. Pain that continues to worsen after 2-3 days, or which is interfering with function, should be checked by a healthcare professional.
