I feel detached or overwhelmed

If you feel a sense of being checked out, not really present, or like things are shifting around you while practicing mindfulness, gently stop and open your eyes.

Use the 5-4-3-2-1 technique from the intro video to ground yourself: name 5 things you can see, 4 things you can feel or touch, 3 things you can hear, 2 things you can taste and 1 thing you can smell.

Try squeezing something (e.g. your wallet or water bottle) to activate your muscles, and increase your sense of touch and connection to your surroundings.

You may prefer to try mindfulness while keeping your eyes open.

These tips can also help if you experience unpleasant images or memories while practicing mindfulness. And remember, help is always available.
