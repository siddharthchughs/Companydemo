I have chronic health issues

If you have an injury or illness, it’s important to talk to a health professional before commencing a new exercise regime.

Speak to your GP or an exercise specialist. Getting tailored support is also linked to better outcomes over the long term.

If you use inhalers or any other medication to control a condition, such as asthma, that can be affected by physical activity, make sure that they’re in-date and that you’ve got them to hand every time you exercise.
