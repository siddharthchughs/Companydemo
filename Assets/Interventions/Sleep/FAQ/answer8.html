What is insomnia?

Insomnia is when sleep problems occur at least three nights per week, for more than three months.

Insomnia can include difficulty falling asleep, waking during the night or early morning and not being able to fall back asleep, or poor sleep quality.

Good sleep hygiene is the first step in addressing sleep problems, but long-term problems may require additional strategies like cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT) for insomnia or stress management techniques. Talk to your GP for more info.
